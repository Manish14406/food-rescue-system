How It Works

Our simple three-step process connects surplus food with those who need it most.

1

Donate

Hotels, restaurants, and event organizers list surplus food on the platform with quantity and pickup details.

2

Request

NGOs and communities browse available meals and claim donations that match their needs.

3

Deliver

Verified delivery partners pick up the food and complete safe, real-time tracked distribution.

RT

Real-Time Updates

Track every step from donation to delivery with live updates for donors and recipients.

VP

Verified Partners

All NGOs and delivery partners are verified for transparency, food safety, and trust.

FD

Fast Delivery

Rapid coordination helps food reach people quickly before the freshness window closes.

IT

Impact Tracking

See how many meals were saved, where they went, and how your donation made a difference.
